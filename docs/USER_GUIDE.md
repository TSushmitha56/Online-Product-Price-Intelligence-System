## 1. Getting Started

Open your web browser and navigate to:

```
https://your-domain.com    (production)
http://localhost:5173       (local development)
```

You'll see the **Login page**. New users can click **Create Account** to register.

---

## 2. Creating an Account

1. Click **Create Account** on the Login page
2. Fill in your details:
   - **First Name** and **Last Name**
   - **Username** (your display name)
   - **Email address** (used to log in)
   - **Password** (minimum 8 characters)
   - **Confirm Password**
3. Click **Register**
4. You'll be automatically logged in and taken to the Home page

> 💡 **Tip:** Use a strong password with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.

---

## 3. Logging In

1. Enter your **Email** and **Password**
2. Click **Sign In**
3. You'll be taken to the Home page

**Forgot your password?**
1. Click **Forgot Password?** on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your inbox for a reset link (valid for 1 hour)
4. Click the link and enter a new password

## 4. Uploading a Product Image

This is the core feature of PriceIntel — take a photo of any product and find the best price!

### Steps:

1. Click **Upload Image** from the Home page or navigation bar
2. Click the **upload area** or drag and drop your image
3. Supported formats: **JPEG, PNG, WebP** (max 10 MB)
4. The system will:
   - ✅ Upload your image securely
   - 🤖 Use AI to identify the product
   - 🔍 Search Amazon, eBay, and Walmart simultaneously
5. Wait a few seconds for results to appear

### Tips for best results:

| ✅ Do | ❌ Avoid |
|---|---|
| Clear, well-lit photo of the product | Blurry or dark photos |
| Product fills most of the frame | Product is tiny in the background |
| Photo taken straight-on | Extreme angles |
| Product without packaging | Very complex scenes |

## 5. Viewing Price Comparison Results

After uploading an image (or running a text search), you'll see the **Results page** with:

### What you'll see:

- **Product Identified**: What the AI thinks the product is (with confidence %)
- **Best Deal Banner**: The lowest price found, highlighted in green
- **Price Summary**:
  - Lowest price
  - Highest price
  - Average price
  - Number of results found

### Result Cards (one per offer):

| Element | Description |
|---|---|
| Product title | Name of the listing |
| Platform badge | Amazon / eBay / Walmart |
| Price | Current listed price |
| Rating | Star rating (if available) |
| Availability | In Stock / Limited / Out of Stock |
| Shipping | Free or estimated shipping cost |
| **View Deal** button | Opens retailer page in new tab |
| **❤️ Wishlist** button | Saves item to your wishlist |
| **⊕ Compare** button | Adds to comparison basket |

### 🏷️ "Best Deal" Badge
The offer with the lowest total cost (price + shipping) is marked with a **Best Deal** badge automatically.

## 6. Text-Based Search

Don't have an image? Search by product name:

1. From the **Home** page, type a product name in the search bar
2. Press **Enter** or click the search icon
3. The system searches all platforms and shows results
4. Results appear progressively as each platform responds

**Example searches:** `Sony WH-1000XM5`, `iPhone 15 case`, `Air fryer 5 quart`

---

## 7. Adding Items to Your Wishlist

Save products you're interested in for later:

1. On any result card, click the **❤️** (heart) icon
2. The item is saved to your Wishlist
3. Access your Wishlist from the navigation menu

### Managing your Wishlist:

- Click **Wishlist** in the navigation bar
- Each saved item shows: name, store, price, and a link to buy
- Click the **🗑️ Remove** button to delete an item
- Click **View Deal** to open the retailer page

> 💡 **Tip:** Prices shown on your Wishlist are from when you saved the item. The actual current price may be different.

## 8. Setting Price Alerts

Get notified when a product drops below your target price:

### How to create an alert:

1. Navigate to **Price Alerts** from the navigation bar
2. Click **+ New Alert**
3. Fill in:
   - **Product Name** — what to monitor (e.g., "Sony WH-1000XM5")
   - **Target Price** — the price you want to be notified at (e.g., $250)
   - **Product URL** _(optional)_ — link to the specific product
4. Click **Create Alert**

### Alert Statuses:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 🟢 **Active** | Price monitoring is running |
| 🔴 **Triggered** | Target price was reached — check now! |
| ⏸️ **Paused** | You've temporarily disabled this alert |

### Managing alerts:

- Click **Edit** to change the target price
- Click **Delete** to remove an alert
- The system checks prices automatically every night

## 9. Using the Dashboard

The Dashboard gives you an overview of your activity and price trends.

**Access:** Click **Dashboard** in the navigation bar

### Dashboard Sections:

#### 📊 Overview Cards
- **Total Searches** — How many searches you've performed
- **Active Alerts** — Price alerts currently running
- **Wishlist Items** — Products saved to your wishlist
- **Searches Today** — Today's activity count

#### 📈 Price History Chart
- Interactive line chart showing price changes over time
- Select a product from your history to see its price trend
- Compare prices from different stores on the same chart

#### 🕑 Recent Searches
- List of your recent product searches
- Click any item to re-run the search

#### 💡 Recommendations
- Products suggested based on your search history

#### 🔔 Recent Alerts
- Status of your most recent price alerts

## 10. Comparing Products Side-by-Side

Compare up to 3 products at once:

1. On the results page, click **⊕ Compare** on up to 3 result cards
2. A **Comparison Basket** appears at the bottom of the screen
3. When ready, click **Compare Now**
4. A side-by-side overlay shows all details for comparison:
   - Price
   - Platform
   - Rating
   - Availability
   - Shipping

> 💡 **Tip:** Compare the same product across Amazon, eBay, and Walmart to find the real best deal.
